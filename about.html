Our Story

Three generations of authentic Lebanese hospitality

A Family Tradition Since 1985

Cedar & Gold Lebanese Restaurant began as a dream of the Khalil family, who immigrated to America from the beautiful mountains of Lebanon. What started as a small family kitchen has grown into the city's most beloved Lebanese restaurant, but our commitment to authenticity has never wavered.

Our recipes have been passed down through three generations, each dish crafted with the same love and attention that our grandmother Fatima brought to her kitchen in Beirut. We source the finest ingredients, many imported directly from Lebanon, to ensure every bite transports you to the Mediterranean.

Today, our family continues to personally oversee every aspect of the restaurant. From our homemade hummus prepared fresh daily to our traditional wood-fired grills, we maintain the authentic flavors that have made us a cornerstone of the community for nearly four decades.
